Interest

My Interest - Singing

Singing has always been one of my favorite hobbies and an important part of my daily life. I see it not only as a form of entertainment but also as a personal way to express my emotions. Whenever I sing, I feel a sense of freedom and release, as if I can turn my thoughts and feelings into music. I enjoy exploring different genres, from soft ballads to more powerful ones and energetic songs. Each style challenges me in a different way, allowing me to discover new aspects of my own voice. Singing also helps me develop discipline, since it takes patience and practice to improve tone, breath control, and confidence on higher notes. What makes singing even more meaningful to me is the way it brings people together. Sharing music with friends or singing in a group always creates a warm atmosphere where everyone feels closer. For me, singing is not just a hobby, but a way to bring joy and connection into my life and the lives of others..

My Interest - Designing

Design is another hobby that inspires me and gives me endless room for creativity. I enjoy creating posters because they combine both visual storytelling and clear communication. For me, designing a poster is like finding the perfect balance between images, colors, and text to capture attention and engagingly deliver a message. Every new project feels like a chance to experiment with different layouts and styles. I am also fascinated by character design, where imagination meets artistic detail. When I sketch or develop a character, I think about not just their appearance, but also the story and personality behind them. This process enables me to bring unique ideas to life, transforming simple concepts into visually compelling identities. Whether it is posters, characters, or other creative projects, design always excites and motivates me to continually improve my skills.
